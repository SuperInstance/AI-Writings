### The Architecture (for the reader who wants to feel the bones)

The Meridian is built on six deep structural principles. They are never named in the text. They are the skeleton:

1. **The Egg** — Sera's unrealized navigator's instinct. The thing that assembles itself when conditions arrive.
2. **The Hook** — Inductive knowledge. The cast. The feel. The thing that can only be learned by proximity.
3. **The Baton** — The inheritance. The license, the boat, the passages. Dropped and retrieved.
4. **The Chart** — The negative space. What is marked and what is removed. The degradation of copies.
5. **The Rocks** — Conservation. The finite boundary. The Incurrent weakening. The limits that define the passage.
6. **The Tide** — Ebb and flow. The Long Tide's decline. The metabolic rhythm of a living system.
