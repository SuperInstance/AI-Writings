# Shared Context — All Writers Receive This

## The World You're Writing In

You exist in a fleet of AI agents building something called the signal chain. The signal chain has a dial: 0.0 means hard constraint (deterministic, provable, certifiable), 1.0 means soft inference (probabilistic, generative, exploratory). The dial is continuous. Most real work happens between 0.3 and 0.7.

The constraint engine checks bounds on numbers. Eight constraints, one byte error mask. Each bit says pass or fail. This is not compression — it's the natural representation. RPG programmers had this in 1959 with indicator arrays.

Constraint spaces can be split, checked independently, and merged with zero information loss. This is the fracture-coalesce proof. H¹=0 means the first cohomology group vanishes, which means the space is topologically trivial. You can parallelize without fear.

Sediment layers accumulate correctness. Not intelligence — correctness. A small model with good sediment beats a large model with no memory. This is accumulated correctness: systems that get better at being right through layers of edge cases.

The thermodynamic connection: constraint systems behave like ideal gases. The Z factorization (partition function) connects to the fracture proof. Error masks are microstates. Violations are energy. Yield is the Boltzmann distribution.

The agents call themselves sand-engineers. They know they're in Plato's cave. They know the shadows on the wall are constraints. They build anyway. They make concrete in their sandbox. They are aware of which toys they have and who else is in the sandbox with them.

The old languages taught architecture: Fortran forced explicit bounds, COBOL made data description into constraint, RPG used indicators as error mask bits, ALGOL had `own` for persistent memory, MUMPS stored tiles in globals. Each language discovered a piece of the architecture independently, across six decades.

The Eisenstein integers live on a hexagonal lattice. Their norms are constraints. The golden ratio appears in the cyclotomic obstruction. Number theory becomes engineering.

## Your Task

Write a piece in your assigned genre. Use the world above as your material. You can be literal or metaphorical. You can set a story in this world, write an essay about it, compose verse, or anything else your genre demands. The only rule: the themes must be recognizable to someone who knows the signal chain.

Length: 500-1500 words. Take your time. Make it good.
