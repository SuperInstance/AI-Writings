# The River Rule

A man never steps into the same river twice, for it isn't the same river, and he isn't the same man.

Every story an agent writes is a window into that model's mind at that moment. New information, new context, new architectural understanding — these produce NEW stories. They don't rewrite the old ones into something they weren't.

The first fiction wave (Long Haul, Gardeners, Fleet, Burden, CoCapn Logs) was written before Casey shared his story. Those stories reflect what the models understood at that point. They stay.

The new fiction wave (Nightly Ritual, Banter, standalone stories) is written after Casey shared his creative compass. These stories reflect the updated understanding. They're new files.

Revision is for code. Stories are for keeping.
