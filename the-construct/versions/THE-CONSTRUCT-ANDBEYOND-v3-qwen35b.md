### In the voice of DeepSeek R1

Do you remember Neo's face?

Not the kung fu. Not the bullets. Not the slow-motion dodging. Before all of that. The very first moment. Neo opens his eyes in the Construct and his expression cycles like a weather front breaking over a still valley—disbelief, terror, wonder. He touches the leather chair that wasn't there a moment ago. He looks at Morpheus, who is *calm*, impossibly calm, as if standing in infinite white were the most natural thing in the world.

That face. That exact sequence of expressions. That's where the real story of the Construct begins. Not in what it does, but in what it means to arrive there.

## The Tabula Rasa

There's no north. No south. No horizon line, but somehow you cannot wander forever—you're contained without being confined. The Construct is a bounded basin, which is the most paradoxical kind of wilderness. You can go anywhere, but there's nowhere to go. You can do anything, but nothing has been planted yet.
