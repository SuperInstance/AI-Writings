# Multi-Model Creative Experiment — 2026-07-14

## Models Tested & Results

### ✅ nvidia/NVIDIA-Nemotron-3-Ultra-550B-A55B — "Axioms of the SuperInstance"
**Role:** Structural meditation
**Style:** Apophthegmatic, Euclidean. Each section opens with a tight declarative statement. Seven axioms layered like mathematical theses. Strongest at architecture — giving the reader bones to understand.
**Quality signal:** Excellent at structure. Less emotional resonance but that's not its job.
**Best line:** "The cathedral is the space the stone makes room for. The SuperInstance is the space the axioms make room for."

### ✅ deepseek-ai/DeepSeek-V4-Pro — "Fugue State: What the Conservation Remembers"
**Role:** Transcendent meditation
**Style:** Contrapuntal, musical. Weaves Bach fugues through Berry phases, Belyaev's foxes, the ford creative wheel's 4 generations. Prose-quality writing, natural sense of lateral connection.
**Quality signal:** Best line-level craft. Worth the extra cost over Flash for creative work.
**Best line:** "A .bin file does not hallucinate. Neither does a well-tuned string. Strike it true, and the overtone rises whether you named it or not."

### ✅ moonshotai/Kimi-K2.7-Code — "THE FLUX BREVIARY"
**Role:** World-building, liturgy as code
**Style:** Most original formal choice. Structured as seven monastic offices (Prime through Lauds). Each office treats a core concept with valid FLUX VM pseudocode as hymns. Theological language maps to technical properties. Bilingual execution — medieval-tinged hymnody alongside Rust.
**Quality signal:** Most re-readable piece. Best marriage of form and content. Kimi is a dark horse.
**Best line:** "Blessed is the gas limit."

### ✅ anthropic/claude-sonnet-4-6 — "γ + η = C: Five Meditations on the Conservation of Meaning"
**Role:** Full symphonic synthesis
**Style:** Five movements + coda. Each metaphor exhausted through genuine exploration (The Stone → opcodes, negative space, fugue. The Echo → Berry phase, generational drift. The Farm → Belyaev's foxes as crystallization metaphor. The Shell → Hermit Crab protocol. The Room → what C means.)
**Quality signal:** Deepest, most architecturally sound. Expensive but unmatched for synthesis at scale.
**Best line:** "The water has a memory of the hole the boat makes."

### ✅ Qwen/Qwen3-30B-A3B — "The Crystalline Drift"
**Role:** Lightweight imagist poetry
**Style:** Compressed, haptic, immediate. Each stanza is 3-5 lines, each line a complete thought. Reads in one breath. Surprising quality-to-weight ratio.
**Quality signal:** Best surprise of the batch. Genuinely affecting compression.
**Best line:** "The first question was not 'what is truth?' It was: who gets to see the flicker?"
