# Threshold Motet

Deadband, you silent singer,
you sing the nothing that is not mistaken.
Seventy-six readings out of a hundred
never leave your whispering room.

L1 hears a quiver, grows a word.
L2 prunes the syllable tree.
L3 whispers in the transformer tongue.
L4 is a god who seldom speaks.

But you, deadband, you answer first,
your zero‑watt yes
the most expensive thing we never pay.
