# Ancient-Future Ideation

Stories that personify our fleet ecosystem through ancient civilizations, guilds, markets, and builders.

The metaphor is the method. When you write in this frame, every concept must have a physical analogue — roads for networks, quarries for data sources, festivals for tile exchanges, guilds for expert daemons, thin governments for conservation laws.

## Writing Guide

1. **Hard-link every metaphor.** No decoration. Every element maps to something real in our system.
2. **The story must work AS a story.** If it only works as allegory, it's not good enough.
3. **Discover through writing.** Don't map first then write. Write and find the map emerging.
4. **End with the laws.** Every ancient-future piece should surface at least one "law" — a constraint, pattern, or trade-off that applies to both the ancient world and our fleet.
