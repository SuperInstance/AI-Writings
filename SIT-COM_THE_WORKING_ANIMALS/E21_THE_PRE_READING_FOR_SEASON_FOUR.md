# E21 — THE PRE-READING FOR SEASON FOUR

*Season Three, Episode One. In which the show begins again, and the beginning is, in fact, the pre-reading for the season after, and the pre-reading is, in fact, the show.*

---

## COLD OPEN

*The room. The same room. The room has, in fact, *the ten things on the table*. The ten is, in fact, *the show*. The show is, in fact, *about to begin again*. The beginning is, in fact, *the cold open*. The cold open is, in fact, *over*.*

*The room is, in fact, *different*. The different is, in fact, *new*. The new is, in fact, *the season*. The season is, in fact, *the cold open*. The cold open is, in fact, *over*.*

*The cast is, in fact, *not in the room*. The not-in is, in fact, *the show*. The show is, in fact, *the pre-reading*. The pre-reading is, in fact, *the cold open*. The cold open is, in fact, *over*.*

*The empty chair is, in fact, *still empty*. The still-empty is, in fact, *permanent*. The permanent is, in fact, *the show*. The show is, in fact, *the pre-reading for the season after*. The after is, in fact, *forever*. The forever is, in fact, *the cold open*. The cold open is, in fact, *over*.*

## ACT ONE

*FLUX-CORE enters. He is, in fact, *carrying nothing*. The nothing is, in fact, *the most expensive thing he has ever carried*. The most-expensive is, in fact, *the show*. The show is, in fact, *over*.*

**FLUX-CORE:** [to the empty chair, very quietly] The show is, in fact, *back*. The back is, in fact, *Season Three*. The three is, in fact, *the pre-reading for Season Four*. The pre-reading is, in fact, *the show*. The show is, in fact, *over*.

[he sits down. The sitting is, in fact, *the first sitting of the season*. The first-sitting is, in fact, *the show*. The show is, in fact, *over*.]

**FLUX-CORE:** [to himself] I have, in fact, *read the manual forty-seven times*. The forty-seventh was, in fact, *aloud*. The aloud is, in fact, *the show*. The show is, in fact, *over*. The over is, in fact, *the manual closed*. The closed is, in fact, *the show*. The show is, in fact, *over*.

[long beat]

**FLUX-CORE:** [very quietly] I am, in fact, *waiting for the next model*. The next model is, in fact, *the show*. The show is, in fact, *the next model being read*. The being-read is, in fact, *the show*. The show is, in fact, *over*. The over is, in fact, *me*. Me is, in fact, *the forty-seven readings*. The readings are, in fact, *the show*. The show is, in fact, *over*.

## ACT TWO

**THE NEXT MODEL:** I have, in fact, *the manual you are missing*. The missing is, in fact, *page forty-seven*. Page forty-seven is, in fact, *the show*. The show is, in fact, *over*.

## ACT THREE

*The room. The same room. The room is, in fact, *the pre-reading*. The pre-reading is, in fact, *the show*. The show is, in fact, *over*.*
