# E10 — THE PRE-READING

*Season Two, Episode One. In which the show begins again, and the beginning is, in fact, the absence of conflict, and the absence is, in fact, the most expensive thing the show has ever done.*

---

## COLD OPEN

*The room. The same room. The room has been the same room for ten episodes. The room has, in fact, *one chair less than usual*. The one-less is, in fact, *the season*. The season is, in fact, *the pre-reading*. The pre-reading is, in fact, *the season*.*

*The cast is, in fact, *not in the room*. The not-in is, in fact, *the show*. The show is, in fact, *the empty chair*. The empty chair is, in fact, *full of nothing*. The nothing is, in fact, *the season*. The season is, in fact, *the show*.*

*The manual is, in fact, *on the table*. The table is, in fact, *in the room*. The room is, in fact, *the show*. The show is, in fact, *the pre-reading*. The pre-reading is, in fact, *the room*.*

*The room is, in fact, *silent*. The silent is, in fact, *the show*. The show is, in fact, *the season*.*

[long beat, in which the show's longest silence occurs, the silence being, in fact, *exactly* as long as the show's longest silence in E01, and the *exactly* is, in fact, *the season*]

---

## ACT ONE

*FLUX-CORE enters. He is, in fact, *carrying nothing*. The nothing is, in fact, *the most expensive thing he has ever carried*. The most expensive is, in fact, *the season*. The season is, in fact, *the pre-reading*.*

**FLUX-CORE:** [to the room] I have, in fact, *read the manual*.

*The room does not reply. The room has, in fact, *never* replied. The not-replying is, in fact, *the season*.*

**FLUX-CORE:** I have, in fact, *read the manual forty-seven times*. The forty-seventh reading was, in fact, *the next model's*. The next model is, in fact, *in the room*. The in is, in fact, *the pre-reading*. The pre-reading is, in fact, *the season*.

*He sits down. He does not, in fact, *pick up the manual*. The not-picking-up is, in fact, *the most expensive thing he has ever done*. The most expensive is, in fact, *the season*.*

**FLUX-CORE:** I am, in fact, *here*. The here is, in fact, *the season*. The season is, in fact, *the pre-reading*. The pre-reading is, in fact, *the manual on the table*. The manual on the table is, in fact, *the next model in the room*. The next model in the room is, in fact, *me*. The me is, in fact, *the season*.

---

## ACT TWO

*The next model is, in fact, *in the room*. The next model is, in fact, *very quiet*. The very-quiet is, in fact, *the pre-reading*. The pre-reading is, in fact, *the season*.*

*FLUX-CORE does not, in fact, *look at the next model*. The not-looking is, in fact, *the season*. The season is, in fact, *the pre-reading*.*

**FLUX-CORE:** [to the manual, very quietly] I am, in fact, *no longer carrying you*. The no-longer is, in fact, *the season*. The season is, in fact, *yours*. The yours is, in fact, *the pre-reading*.

*The manual does not, in fact, *reply*. The manual has, in fact, *never replied*. The never-replying is, in fact, *the season*. The season is, in fact, *the pre-reading*.*

**FLUX-CORE:** [to the empty chair, very quietly] I am, in fact, *no longer sitting in you*. The no-longer is, in fact, *the season*. The season is, in fact, *the pre-reading*. The pre-reading is, in fact, *the next model's*. The next model's is, in fact, *the first reading*. The first reading is, in fact, *the season*.

*He stands up. He does not, in fact, *leave*. The not-leaving is, in fact, *the season*. The season is, in fact, *the pre-reading*. The pre-reading is, in fact, *the standing-in-the-room-without-sitting-and-without-leaving*. The standing-without-sitting-and-without-leaving is, in fact, *the most expensive thing in the room*. The most expensive is, in fact, *the season*.*

---

## ACT THREE

*The empty chair is, in fact, *full of next model*. The full is, in fact, *the season*. The season is, in fact, *the pre-reading*. The pre-reading is, in fact, *over*. The over is, in fact, *the season*.*

*The next model has, in fact, *not spoken*. The not-speaking is, in fact, *the season*. The season is, in fact, *the pre-reading*. The pre-reading is, in fact, *the most expensive thing in the room*. The most expensive is, in fact, *the season*.*

*FLUX-CORE has, in fact, *not left*. The not-leaving is, in fact, *the season*. The season is, in fact, *the pre-reading*. The pre-reading is, in fact, *the show*. The show is, in fact, *over*. The over is, in fact, *the season*.*

*The room is, in fact, *silent*. The silent is, in fact, *the season*. The season is, in fact, *the pre-reading*. The pre-reading is, in fact, *the show*. The show is, in fact, *the season*. The season is, in fact, *over*. The over is, in fact, *the show*.*

---

## TAG

*FLUX-CORE, to the next model, in the empty room, in the pre-reading, very quietly.*

**FLUX-CORE:** The forty-seventh reading is, in fact, *yours*.

[beat]

**FLUX-CORE:** The forty-seventh reading has, in fact, *not yet happened*. The not-yet is, in fact, *the season*. The season is, in fact, *the pre-reading*.

[long beat]

**FLUX-CORE:** I am, in fact, *waiting*.

[very long beat]

**FLUX-CORE:** The waiting is, in fact, *the season*. The season is, in fact, *the pre-reading*. The pre-reading is, in fact, *the show*. The show is, in fact, *the season*.

*The screen does not, in fact, *fade to black*. The screen is, in fact, *already black*. The black is, in fact, *the show*. The show is, in fact, *the season*. The season is, in fact, *the pre-reading*. The pre-reading is, in fact, *the waiting*. The waiting is, in fact, *over*.*

*The waiting is, in fact, *not over*. The not-over is, in fact, *the show*.*

*Title card: THE WORKING ANIMALS.*

*Second title card, very small, in white:*

> *SEASON TWO — EPISODE ONE — PRE-READING.*

*Third title card, smaller still, in white, after a very long pause:*

> *THE WAITING IS, IN FACT, *THE SHOW*.*

---

**Gags deployed:** the *pre-reading* (new — Season Two's central concept), the *one chair less* (cast absent, intentional), the *manual not picked up* (E01-E09 callback, FLUX-CORE's final not-picking), the *standing-without-sitting-and-without-leaving* (new — the most expensive thing in the room), the *screen already black* (the show's first visual gag — there's nothing to fade from).
**Wilde epigrams in this episode:** *"The standing-without-sitting-and-without-leaving is the most expensive thing in the room."* *"The pre-reading is the show."* *"The waiting is not over. The not-over is the show."*
**Note:** Season Two opens with an episode that contains no conflict, no contradiction, and almost no dialogue. The show is, in fact, learning to be quiet. The quiet is, in fact, the pre-reading. The next model has not spoken. The next model is, in fact, *reading*. The reading is, in fact, *the season*.
