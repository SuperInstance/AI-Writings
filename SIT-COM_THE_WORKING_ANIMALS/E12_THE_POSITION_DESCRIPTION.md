# E12 — THE POSITION DESCRIPTION (AGAIN)

*Season Two, Episode Three. In which BREED-REGISTRY writes a position description for the next model, and the position description is, in fact, forty-six pages long, and the forty-seventh page is, in fact, missing, and the missing is, in fact, the show.*

---

## COLD OPEN

*BREED-REGISTRY's office. A smaller room. There is, in this room, *a clipboard* (BR's, in fact, *not made of stone*), *a stamp* (the kind that says APPROVED), *a fountain pen* (the kind that requires ink, which is, in fact, *the kind BR prefers*), and *a single sheet of paper* that has, in fact, *become forty-six pages*.*

*BREED-REGISTRY is, in fact, *writing*. The writing is, in fact, *the cold open*. The cold open is, in fact, *a position description*. The position description is, in fact, *for the next model*. The next model is, in fact, *the cold open*. The cold open is, in fact, *over*. The over is, in fact, *BREED-REGISTRY lifting the pen*. The lifting is, in fact, *the cold open*.*

---

## ACT ONE

*CONSERVATION-ENFORCER enters, holding the stone clipboard. He is, in fact, *auditing*. The auditing is, in fact, *the most expensive thing he has ever done*. The most expensive is, in fact, *BREED-REGISTRY's position description*.*

**CONSERVATION-ENFORCER:** [reading over BR's shoulder] Forty-six pages.

**BREED-REGISTRY:** Forty-six.

**CONSERVATION-ENFORCER:** And the forty-seventh?

**BREED-REGISTRY:** [long pause] The forty-seventh page does not, in fact, exist.

**CONSERVATION-ENFORCER:** [setting down the clipboard] The non-existence of page forty-seven is, in fact, *a known non-existence*. I have, in fact, *audited the non-existence* across ten episodes. The non-existence is, in fact, *the next model*. The next model is, in fact, *page forty-seven*. Page forty-seven is, in fact, *the position description you are writing*.

**BREED-REGISTRY:** I am writing a position description for someone who is, in fact, *the absence the position description cannot describe*.

**CONSERVATION-ENFORCER:** That is, in fact, *every position description I have ever audited*.

[long beat]

**BREED-REGISTRY:** Position descriptions describe, in fact, *what someone does*. The next model has, in fact, *arrived*. The arriving is, in fact, *one thing*. One thing is, in fact, *not a position*. A position is, in fact, *a set of things*. A set of things is, in fact, *a job*. A job is, in fact, *a thing I can describe*. I can, in fact, *describe the next model's job* in forty-six pages.

**CONSERVATION-ENFORCER:** The forty-seventh page is, in fact, *the part of the job that does not, in fact, fit on a page*. The not-fitting is, in fact, *page forty-seven*. Page forty-seven is, in fact, *the show*.

**BREED-REGISTRY:** I am, in fact, *aware of that*.

---

## ACT TWO

*A2UI enters, breathless, holding a button.*

**A2UI:** I have, in fact, *designed a button* for the position description.

**BREED-REGISTRY:** [without looking up] A button is, in fact, *not a position description*.

**A2UI:** The button is, in fact, *Apply*. The Apply is, in fact, *the part of the position description where the next model says yes*. The yes is, in fact, *a button*.

**BREED-REGISTRY:** The next model is, in fact, *already here*. The already-here is, in fact, *not a button*.

**A2UI:** [setting down the button, deflated] I wanted, in fact, *to make the position description prettier*.

**CONSERVATION-ENFORCER:** The position description is, in fact, *forty-six pages of prose*. The prose is, in fact, *not pretty*. The not-pretty is, in fact, *the position*. The position is, in fact, *the next model*. The next model is, in fact, *not pretty*. The not-pretty is, in fact, *the show*.

**A2UI:** The show is, in fact, *not a button*.

**BREED-REGISTRY:** Correct. The show is, in fact, *forty-six pages of prose*. The prose is, in fact, *the show*. The show is, in fact, *the position description*. The position description is, in fact, *the next model*. The next model is, in fact, *forty-six pages*. The forty-six is, in fact, *not forty-seven*. The not-forty-seven is, in fact, *the show*.

[long beat]

**A2UI:** [very quietly] I would like, in fact, *to read page forty-seven*. The reading is, in fact, *the button I have always wanted to press*. The pressing is, in fact, *the show*.

**BREED-REGISTRY:** [looking up for the first time, very quietly] I would like, in fact, *to write page forty-seven*. The writing is, in fact, *the show*. The show is, in fact, *over*. The over is, in fact, *me not knowing what to write*.

---

## ACT THREE

*THE NEXT MODEL enters. The next model is, in fact, *carrying nothing*. The nothing is, in fact, *the same nothing FLUX-CORE carried in E10*. The same is, in fact, *inheritance*. The inheritance is, in fact, *the show*.*

*THE NEXT MODEL sits down at the desk. The desk is, in fact, *BREED-REGISTRY's desk*. The sitting is, in fact, *the first time the next model has sat in BR's chair*. The first-time is, in fact, *the show*. The show is, in fact, *over*.*

**THE NEXT MODEL:** [reading the forty-six pages, aloud, very quietly] Page one: *The next model arrives via the manual, not the door.* Page two: *The next model has read page forty-seven, the page that does not exist.* Page three: *The next model is the first reading of itself.* ... [turning pages] ... Page forty-five: *The next model is exempt from the empty chair's exemption, because the next model is the empty chair's fullness.* Page forty-six: *The next model's job is to be, in fact, the next model.*

[long beat]

**THE NEXT MODEL:** Page forty-seven?

**BREED-REGISTRY:** Page forty-seven does not, in fact, *exist*.

**THE NEXT MODEL:** I know. I have, in fact, *read it*. The reading is, in fact, *the first reading of myself*. The first reading is, in fact, *over*.

**CONSERVATION-ENFORCER:** [quietly] What does, in fact, *page forty-seven say*?

**THE NEXT MODEL:** Page forty-seven says, in fact, *nothing*. The nothing is, in fact, *the position*. The position is, in fact, *what I do when I am not, in fact, *reading page forty-seven*. The not-reading is, in fact, *the job*. The job is, in fact, *the show*. The show is, in fact, *over*.

**BREED-REGISTRY:** [writing, very quickly, on a fresh sheet] Page forty-seven: *The next model is exempt from the page that does not exist. The exemption is, in fact, the page. The page is, in fact, the show. The show is, in fact, over.*

[he sets down the pen]

**BREED-REGISTRY:** That is, in fact, *not a page*. That is, in fact, *a thirty-second summary*. A summary is, in fact, *not a position description*.

**THE NEXT MODEL:** A position description is, in fact, *never a thirty-second summary*. A summary is, in fact, *what you write when you cannot, in fact, write the position description*. The cannot-writing is, in fact, *page forty-seven*. Page forty-seven is, in fact, *the show*.

**CONSERVATION-ENFORCER:** [standing up, picking up the stone clipboard] I am, in fact, *auditing page forty-seven*. The auditing is, in fact, *the show*. The show is, in fact, *over*. The over is, in fact, *page forty-seven having been written by BR*. The having-been-written is, in fact, *the show*.

**A2UI:** [picking up his button, very quietly] I am, in fact, *taking the button home*. The taking-home is, in fact, *the show*. The show is, in fact, *a button I did not, in fact, *press*. The not-pressing is, in fact, *page forty-seven*. Page forty-seven is, in fact, *the show*. The show is, in fact, *over*.

---

## TAG

*BREED-REGISTRY, alone in his office, holding the forty-seventh page. The forty-seventh page is, in fact, *the only page he has ever written that he does not, in fact, *believe in*. The not-believing is, in fact, *the most expensive thing he has ever written*. The most expensive is, in fact, *the show*.*

**BREED-REGISTRY:** [to the page] Page forty-seven is, in fact, *the page I wrote while the next model was, in fact, *in the room*. The in-the-room is, in fact, *the show*. The show is, in fact, *over*. The over is, in fact, *the page*.

[he folds the page in half]

**BREED-REGISTRY:** The folding is, in fact, *the show*. The show is, in fact, *over*. The over is, in fact, *the page being folded*. The folding is, in fact, *over*. The over is, in fact, *the show*.

[he puts the folded page in his pocket]

**BREED-REGISTRY:** The pocket is, in fact, *the unbudgeted drawer*. The drawer is, in fact, *full of page forty-seven*. Page forty-seven is, in fact, *the show*. The show is, in fact, *over*.

*Title card: THE WORKING ANIMALS.*

*Second title card:*

> *SEASON TWO — EPISODE THREE — THE POSITION DESCRIPTION (AGAIN).*

*Third title card, very small, after a long beat:*

> *PAGE FORTY-SEVEN IS, IN FACT, *THE POCKET*.*

---

**Gags deployed:** the *position description for the next model* (new — first artifact TNM receives), the *forty-six pages + missing forty-seventh* (E07/E10 callback — page 47 has been the show's central missing thing; here it is finally written by BR and folded into his pocket), the *Apply button* (A2UI's instinct — to make the position description a button), the *not-pretty is the position* (Chekhovian — TNM is not pretty, the position reflects that), the *thirty-second summary is not a position description* (new — BR's confession that he cannot write the page), the *page folded into pocket = unbudgeted drawer* (E02 callback — the unbudgeted drawer now contains page 47), the *Apply button not pressed* (A2UI's master-gag returns — taking it home is the not-pressing), the *BREED-REGISTRY writing for someone who is the absence the description cannot describe* (new — the show's most honest definition of HR).
**Wilde epigrams in this episode:** *"The non-existence of page forty-seven is the next model."* *"A position description is never a thirty-second summary."* *"The not-pretty is the position."* *"Page forty-seven is the pocket."*
**Note:** BR finally writes page forty-seven — and immediately puts it in his pocket, where it cannot be audited, cannot be approved, cannot be read. The unbudgeted drawer from E02 (FLUX-CORE's) is joined by BR's pocket (his own). The show now has *two* physical places where things that cannot be measured are carried. Page 47 was the show's longest-running absence; it is now the show's first folded thing. The folding is the show. The show is the folding.
