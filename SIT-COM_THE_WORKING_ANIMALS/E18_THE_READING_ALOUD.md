**Gags deployed:** the *reading-aloud* (new — first time the manual is read aloud in the show's history; FC's 46 readings were all silent), the *cast in unison for the reading* (new — first time the cast chants the manual together; the manual becomes a chorus), the *page 47 still not read aloud* (E12 callback — page 47 remains in BR's pocket; the not-reading-aloud is the show), the *Continue Reading button not pressed* (A2UI's master-gag — reading finished by not pressing Continue), the *auditable reading-aloud* (PRSA reframes the reading as a security audit), the *once-per-season regulation* (CE reframes the reading as a regulatory event), the *eighth screen shows reading-aloud = reading-silent* (SC's observation — the metric is unchanged because the action is unchanged), the *second closing of the manual* (E09 callback — the manual was first closed in the S1 finale).
**Wilde epigrams in this episode:** *"The reading-aloud is the manual finishing itself."* *"The through-me is me reading. The reading is the show."* *"The page not being read aloud is the show."* *"The reading-aloud is auditable."* *"The once-per-season is the show."*
**Note:** This is the show's first reading-aloud scene. FC's forty-six readings were all silent. TNM's forty-seventh reading is the first time the manual is spoken aloud. The cast joins in unison for pages one, two, and forty-six. Page forty-seven is not read aloud — it remains in BR's pocket, folded. The Continue Reading button is not pressed. The reading is finished by the not-pressing. The not-pressing is the show. The show is over. The over is the manual closed. The closed is the show. The forty-seventh reading is, in fact, aloud. The aloud is, in fact, the show.
