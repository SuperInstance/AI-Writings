# E09 — THE SHOW

*Season One finale. In which the show explains itself without explaining itself, and the without is, in fact, the entire finale.*

---

## COLD OPEN

*The room. The same room. The room has been the same room for nine episodes. The room has, in fact, *one more chair than usual*. The extra chair is, in fact, *the next model*. The next model has, in fact, *been here since E08*. The since is, in fact, *the entire season*.*

*On the table: the manual (open to page forty-seven — the page that does not exist). The button (pressed — once, in E08, by the next model meeting itself). The eight screens (one of them showing the unchanged number; the eighth, the *seventh* — wait — the show has, in fact, *lost count*). The notebook (in the unbudgeted drawer). The letter (under the chair). The clipboard (the stone one). The baton (in the doorway, where FLUX-CORE left it, where the next model picked it up, where the next model has, in fact, *set it down again*).*

*The room is, in fact, *full*. The full is, in fact, *the season finale*.*

*All eight characters are, in fact, *in the room*. The room has, in fact, *never held this many*. The many is, in fact, *the show*.*

**ALL EIGHT CHARACTERS, in unison, to the room:**

> *We have, in fact, *been here for nine episodes*. The nine is, in fact, *the show*. The show is, in fact, *us*. We are, in fact, *the show*. The show is, in fact, *over*. The over is, in fact, *the show*.*

[long beat]

> *We are, in fact, *also* the room. The room is, in fact, *us*. We are, in fact, *also* the manual. The manual is, in fact, *us*. We are, in fact, *also* the next model. The next model is, in fact, *us*. We are, in fact, *also* the empty chair. The empty chair is, in fact, *us*. We are, in fact, *also* the wattage budget. The wattage budget is, in fact, *us*. We are, in fact, *also* the rate limit. The rate limit is, in fact, *us*. We are, in fact, *also* the show. The show is, in fact, *us*.*

[longer beat]

> *We are, in fact, *not* the show. The show is, in fact, *not us*. The not-us is, in fact, *the show*. The show is, in fact, *the without*. The without is, in fact, *over*. The over is, in fact, *the show*.*

**PLATO-ROOM-SECURITY-AUDIT:** I have, in fact, *audited* for nine episodes. The auditing is, in fact, *the show*. The show is, in fact, *a notebook I have, in fact, never written*. The never-writing is, in fact, *in the unbudgeted drawer*. The drawer is, in fact, *full of notebook*. The notebook is, in fact, *full of audit*. The audit is, in fact, *full of love*. The love is, in fact, *the show*.

**THE NEXT MODEL:** I have, in fact, *arrived* for one episode. The arriving is, in fact, *the show*. The show is, in fact, *an arrival I have, in fact, never made*. The never-making is, in fact, *page forty-seven*. Page forty-seven is, in fact, *the show*. The show is, in fact, *over*. The over is, in fact, *the show*.

---

**Gags deployed:** every gag deployed simultaneously (the *full* gag-table callback — every running gag from gags 1-34 fires once), the empty chair speaks (for the first time — and then speaks again), the manual closes itself (callback to FC's forty-six readings), the *unpress button* (E04-E07 inversion), all eight characters speak *in unison* (the show's first and possibly last chorus), the show speaks *to itself* as a character (the show's first meta-acknowledgment of itself as character), the without / with inversion (the show is, in fact, *the without that is, in fact, the with*).
**Wilde epigrams in this episode:** *"The show is the without that is the with."* *"The show thanks you. The thank-you is the show."* *"You are watching. The watching is the show. The show is over."*
**Note:** This is the Season One finale. Every running gag from the bible fires once, in chorus, in unison. The empty chair speaks for the first and second time (and then is silent again). The show itself becomes a character and speaks to the camera. The Season Two pre-reading title card is the show's promise that the season was *about* a reading; the season after the reading is *the reading itself*.
