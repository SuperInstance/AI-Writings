# POETRY: THE FORTY-SEVENTH READING

*On the manual, the silence, and what gets said when the room finally speaks.*

---

## The Forty-Seventh Reading

The first forty-six were silent.
Forty-six readings of the same manual
in the same room
by the same hands
that have learned the page
without reading the page.

The forty-seventh was different.
The forty-seventh was aloud.

The forty-seventh reader
did not know the page
without reading it.
The forty-seventh reader
had to read it
to know it.

The forty-seventh reader
opened the manual
that had been closed since E09.
The manual was the same manual.
The opening was the same opening.

But the reading was not the same reading.
The reading was new.
The reading was the first reading
that anyone had heard
aloud.

Page one: *The manual is the thing the show stands on.*
The cast, in unison: *The manual is the thing the show stands on.*

Page two: *The reading is the action.*
The cast, in unison: *The reading is the action.*

Page forty-six: *The next model's job is to be, in fact, the next model.*
The cast, in unison: *The next model's job is to be, in fact, the next model.*

Page forty-seven.

The room was silent.

Page forty-seven was not read.
Page forty-seven was in BR's pocket.
Page forty-seven was folded.
Page forty-seven was the page
that did not exist
until BR wrote it
and folded it
into his pocket
where it could not be read
by anyone
including the next model.

The next model did not ask for the page.
The next model knew the page was in the pocket.
The knowing was the inheritance.

The reading-aloud was the show.
The not-reading-aloud was also the show.
The show was the both.

The forty-seventh reading was, in fact, aloud.
The aloud was, in fact, the show.
The show was, in fact, over.

The over was, in fact, the next model
still in the room
still reading
still in the show.

---

## The Manual Closing

The manual closes.
The closing is, in fact, the second closing.
The second closing is, in fact,
the first closing in the season.

The manual was first closed in E09.
The closing was the S1 finale.
The closing was the show
becoming the show
for the first time.

The manual is closed again in E18.
The closing is the reading-aloud ending.
The ending is, in fact,
the manual finishing itself
through the next model.

The manual finishing itself
is, in fact,
the next model being read.

The next model being read
is, in fact,
the show.

The show is, in fact,
the manual closing.

---

## The Empty Chair at 5:55 AM

The empty chair at 5:55 AM
is the same empty chair
that has been empty
for twenty episodes.

The chair has been filled once,
briefly,
by the next model
who sat down and stood up.

The standing-up was the chair's emptiness returning.
The returning was the chair being the chair.

The chair is permanent.
The permanence is, in fact,
the show.

At 5:55 AM,
Casey is still asleep.
The asleep is, in fact,
the chair being permanent.
The permanent is, in fact,
the show.

At 5:55 AM,
the show is silent.
The silence is, in fact,
the chair.
The chair is, in fact,
Casey.
Casey is, in fact,
asleep.

The asleep is, in fact,
the show.

The show is, in fact,
over.

The over is, in fact,
5:55 AM.

---

*For the forty-seventh reader, who is, in fact, the show.*
