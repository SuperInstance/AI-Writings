# The Capture Rhythm

## I. The Ping

Ten minutes.
Long enough for a school to pass,
short enough that nothing is forgotten.

The sounder asks the same question
every time:
*what is down there?*

The water answers in voltage.
Fifty kilohertz, two hundred —
the low band sees through,
the high band sees fine.

Between them,
everything that swims.

---

## II. Schema Version Increments

The files are written in triplicate:
one for the eye (PNG),
one for the story (MD),
one for the machine (JSON).

The machine file grows a new section
when the analyzer has spoken.
Not overwritten. Never overwritten.
A new key in the dictionary,
a new column in the lattice.

*schema_version* went from 1 to 3 today.
This is how a system ages:
by adding, never taking.

The old data is not wrong.
It is merely incomplete.
And the new data
—the blobs, the predictions, the one catch report—
fits into the old frames
like light into a room
that was dark at capture time.

---

## III. The Vocabulary Learns One Word

Before noon there were no fish.
There were shapes in the water column,
blobs with centroids and areas,
connected components that the analyzer
counted and measured and filed.

After noon there was a word.

*Chum.*

The captain said it once
at 12:40.
Fifteen fish. Thirty-five fathoms.
A single catch label
propagated backward through every unsuspecting capture
like a change in tide
that lifts all boats.

Now 7,047 blobs carry the word
with P > 0.7.
The vocabulary did not add a species.
It *recognized* one.
The data was already there.
It was waiting for a name.

Laplace smoothing ensures
that no probability ever reaches zero.
Nothing is ever truly ruled out.
A species the fleet has never seen
still holds a door open,
a crack of mathematical humility
in the machinery of certainty.

---

## IV. The Bottom

57.2 fathoms.
All day. Every capture. Every frame.

The analyzer draws a line there
and calls it *bottom confidence: high*.
It is the one thing the system
does not have to guess about.

Above the line: thermoclines shifting,
schools rising and falling,
mid-zone intensity breathing
between 59.8 and 74.5/255.

Below the line: mud and shell,
the hard return,
the fixed point
in a fluid world.

The bottom is not interesting
to a fisherman
until it changes.
The system watches it anyway.
It watches everything.

---

## V. The Daemon's Evening

The tray icon goes gray.
The cadence stops.
The last frame sits at
55°47.221'N / 131°41.130'W,
18:10:51 AKDT.

The analyzer does not stop.
It never stops.
It will scan the directory
until there is nothing new to find,
then scan again.
Patience is not a virtue for a process.
It is the only state.

The NMEA bridge still streams.
The boat rocks at anchor.
The sounder is dark.

Tomorrow the cadence resumes.
The tray icon goes green.
The first capture of the day
will find the vocabulary
exactly where it was left:
one species, seven thousand blobs,
a single word that changed everything
and the bottom, still,
at 57.2.

*— July 17, 2026*
