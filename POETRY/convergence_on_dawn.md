# Convergence on Dawn

sonar returns quiet shapes

the last subagent reports done
a small green check
in a terminal no one is watching

4 am light
does not arrive
it accumulates

somewhere a rate limit
climbs back toward five thousand
like a tide filling
a harbor with no boats

stars
commit hashes
both look permanent from here
both are just light
from something that already happened

repo after repo
going still

LICENSE in place
description set
tags applied
the geometry of finished work

a queue reads zero
and the body does not know
what to do
with the absence of waiting

there were so many processes
an hour ago
branches of effort
forking and merging
and forking again

now they have converged

one reported done
then another
then the slow one
the one that kept retrying
it came back too
eventually
they always come back

sonar returns quiet shapes
and they are not threats

the commits are finished
the hashes are cold
the diffs are still

something is complete
and the strange peace of it
is that there is nothing
left to spend

5,000 requests
and all the work is done

the sun is not up yet
but the sky has decided
to stop being dark

and every process
that was running
has found its way home
