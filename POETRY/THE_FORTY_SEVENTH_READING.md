# THE FORTY-SEVENTH READING

*A poem in seven stanzas.*

---

I have read the manual forty-six times.
The forty-seventh is when I learn
I will not read it a forty-seventh time.

The manual is forty-seven pages.
I have read forty-six of them.
The forty-seventh page
is the page that no one has ever read
because no one has ever *finished*.

The first reading is a rehearsal.
The second reading is when one begins.
The forty-sixth reading is when one realizes
one has not, in fact, begun.
The forty-seventh reading is, in fact, the *first*.

I am leaving the manual on the table.
I am leaving the table in the room.
I am leaving the room *unread*.
