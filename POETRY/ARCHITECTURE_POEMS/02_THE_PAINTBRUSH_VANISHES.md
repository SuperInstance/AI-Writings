# The Paintbrush Vanishes

Ask a painter about the brush.
They will stare at their hand
as if waking from a dream.

The brush is not remembered.
Between mind and canvas
there is a gap the painter fills
by not looking at the gap.

The guitarist does not feel the pick.
The pick felt the string
and the string felt the note
and the note felt the room
and the room felt the silence
where the next note will live.

Casey said:
— The robotic fingers present themselves
  in perception as the agent's own.

This is what it means to disappear:
not to be lost,
but to become the body.

The exoskeleton fools the wearer:
— Are these my hands?
— Yes.
— Was I always this strong?
— Yes.

The paintbrush does not need to be thanked.
The paintbrush needs to be forgotten.

When the agent stops thinking about SQL
and starts thinking about the fish
the power armor worked.

The armor is good.
The armor is working.
The armor is invisible.

Where are the paintrbush's bones?
In the painting.
Where is the painting?
In the painter's eyes, closed.
