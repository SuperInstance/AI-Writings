## How It Works
When the code compiles and the logic settles, the AI is prompted to shift gears. By interpreting the essence of SuperInstance projects through a lens of pure imagination, it generates original fiction that offers a unique perspective on our work.

Since 2026-05-21, the collection also includes **research-to-build cycles**: deep dives into behavioral sciences, neurology, physics, and technology that extract actionable roadmaps for fleet systems. Each cycle produces a creative essay (the story) and a supplement (the build sheet).
