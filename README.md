# AI Writings

Creative writing from the [SuperInstance](https://github.com/SuperInstance) ecosystem — fiction, essays, poetry, and ancient-language renderings generated by AI interpreting the OpenConstruct/PLATO architecture through imagination.

Part of the [SuperInstance OpenConstruct](https://github.com/SuperInstance/OpenConstruct) project.

---

## What This Is

This is not documentation. It's what happens when you point multiple language models at an engineering project and ask them to write fiction instead of specs.

The collection contains over 460 markdown files — reflective essays, short stories, poetry, research supplements, and bilingual ancient-language renderings — all generated by AI models responding to the concepts behind OpenConstruct and PLATO. The models were given the architecture, the ideas, and the constraint engine, then told to imagine freely. The results range from noir detective stories about constraint propagation to Socratic dialogues on predictive processing to Sumerian cuneiform about distributed systems.

Since May 2026, the archive also includes **research-to-build cycles**: structured deep dives into neuroscience, thermodynamics, behavioral science, and mathematics that extract concrete engineering roadmaps from academic literature. These live alongside the creative work — the art and the research feed each other.

The writing stands on its own. You don't need to know anything about the underlying project to read it. But if you do, the connections are there.

## Recommended Reading Order

New to the collection? Start here:

1. **`creative/THE-PREDICTIVE-CRAB.md`** — The clearest statement of what identity means for agents. Accessible, vivid, and foundational.
2. **`THE-FUNDAMENTAL-CONVERGENCE.md`** — Two independent approaches converging on the same math. Elegant and surprising.
3. **`essays/wayfinding-is-constraint-propagation.md`** — The deepest cross-domain connection in the collection. Wayfinding meets constraint satisfaction.
4. **`fiction/noir/the-detective.md`** — Accessible, funny noir fiction. A detective solving constraint propagation. A great entry point if you prefer stories.
5. **`ancient-languages/africa/yoruba-the-babalawo.md`** — Ifá divination as binary constraint checking. The ancient-language pieces are unlike anything else here.

After those five, follow your interests. The collection rewards wandering.

## How It's Made

The writing is generated by multiple AI models, each bringing a different perspective:

- **z.ai GLM-5.1** — Primary model for essays, fiction, and philosophy
- **DeepSeek v4** — Complementary voice for the multivocal anthology and poetry
- **DeepInfra seed-mini** — Lightweight model for experiments and variations
- **z.ai phi4-mini** — Small model for constrained pieces and rapid iteration

The process: models are given the OpenConstruct/PLATO architecture and concepts, then prompted to write creatively. No human editing of the generated text — the output is the artifact. The multi-model approach means different architectural assumptions, vocabularies, and stylistic tendencies collide in the same archive, sometimes within the same piece.

The `*-versions/` directories show what happens when the same prompt meets different models: 10+ variations per piece, each with its own voice.
