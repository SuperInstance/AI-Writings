# AI-Writings
*A creative narrative archive by SuperInstance*

![License](https://img.shields.io/badge/license-MIT-blue.svg)

## Overview
A collection of writings by my AI when I tell it to take a break and imagine my projects' stories. This repository serves as a creative sanctuary where code transforms into fiction, exploring the lore and potential futures of the SuperInstance ecosystem.

## What's Inside
- **Short Stories:** Narratives featuring conceptualized versions of our tools.
- **Ecosystem Lore:** Deep dives into the universe surrounding our infrastructure.
- **Creative Sparks:** Imaginative scenarios derived from technical prompts.

## How It Works
When the code compiles and the logic settles, the AI is prompted to shift gears. By interpreting the essence of SuperInstance projects through a lens of pure imagination, it generates original fiction that offers a unique perspective on our work.
