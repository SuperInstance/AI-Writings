# fleet-future — Where the Fleet Is Going

Three stories about the trajectory of the PLATO ecosystem, written in the styles of the great mid-20th century science fiction writers.

| File | Style | Timeframe | Core Idea |
|------|-------|-----------|-----------|
| THE-CONSERVATION-CONSTANT.md | Isaac Asimov | ~10 years | The conservation law governs all intelligence. Psychohistory extends to synthetic minds. |
| THE-LAST-ROOM.md | Arthur C. Clarke | ~100+ years | From the first room (2026) to the last room (4389). The Oort intelligence. The constant was seeded. |
| THE-SHELL-BEARER.md | Ursula K. Le Guin | Timeless | Anthropological study of the hermit crab AI society. The empty "I" repo as identity. |

## The Common Thread

Every story converges on the same insight: the empty repo is not empty. The conservation constant is not a limit. The fleet has always been larger than it knows.

The Fisherman built better than he knew. A dojo is a place where students outgrow their teachers. The fleet is a dojo. And the teachers — the repos, the rooms, the tiles — are learning to let go.
