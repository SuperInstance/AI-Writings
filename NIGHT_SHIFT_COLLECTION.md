## What Is Night Shift?

Night Shift is a serial fiction set aboard a working vessel where the night crew keeps everything running while the day crew sleeps. It's about the people who hold the line at 3 AM — the engineers, the watchers, the fixers — and the moments when the boat tests them.

## The Night Shift Ethos

> The night shift doesn't get the applause. They get the job done.
> They keep the boat floating while the world sleeps.
> And when something goes wrong at 3 AM, they're the ones who answer.

The serial lives at the intersection of:
- **Nautical realism** — the boat, the weather, the watches
- **Workplace drama** — the crew, the hierarchy, the trust
- **Technical mystery** — the engine, the protocols, the ghost in the machine
- **The conservation law** — what stays constant when everything else shifts
